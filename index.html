Topic: Brainstorming Session (Initial Ideas for the Project)

The purpose of this meeting is so that we can talk about what the project will be about and our initial impressions of it.
We will use this time to get to know our team members and get a feel for what they're all about.
We will be discussing topics like courses they have taken, what their interests are, how we would be able to communicate with each other, among other things.
By the end of the meeting, the hope is that we'll all be on the same page and ready to tackle the project together with a clear goal in sight.

Agenda

  1. Discuss the project and how we feel about it.
  2. Cover the basics that needs to be done.
  3. Set deadlines for ourselves to be able to meet and plan.
  4. Talk about communication methods that we'll be using.
  5. Assign roles for each group member so we have a rough idea of who will do what.
